1. AICE

Something that a person might say when they agree with something or is happy about something

2. Beta/Beti

Something that a person calls someone that is younger than them. Beta for boys and Beti fo girls

3. BHAT

A softer version of DISHKOWMN (see DISHKOWMN)

4. DISH

Abbreviation of DISHKOWMN (see DISHKOWMN)

5. DISHKOWMN

A very ancient martial art style created by indian movies and then adapteed by the Major DISHKOWMN Gods(Tanish, Kaushal, Amrith)

6. FHAT-BHAT

Harder that BHAT but softer than DISHKOWMN (see BHAT, see DISHKOWMN)

7. Gadu

Something that a boy might call another boy who is younger or is the same age as them

8. NOT ANYMORE!

Something that someone might say to counter someone elses insult or to cancel a verb

9. noAICE

The opposite of AICE (see AICE)

10. oh yes

Something that Beta Ji said and somewhat turned into a meme. this later evolved into AICE (see AICE)
